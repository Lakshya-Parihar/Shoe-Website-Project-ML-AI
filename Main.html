Customer's Queries

What is your shipping policy?

Shoezzz offers free standard shipping on all orders above ₹500. For orders below ₹500, a nominal shipping fee of ₹50 will be charged.

Do you have a physical store?

Not yet! For now, we are exclusively an online store, bringing amazing shoes right to your doorstep.

What's your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase, provided the shoes are unworn and in their original packaging.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we do! Shipping costs for international orders will be calculated at checkout based on your location.

What payment options are available?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay and PayPal.

Can I change my order?

Sure! Just contact our customer support within an hour of placing your order, and we'll sort it out.
